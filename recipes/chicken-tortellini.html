Chicken Mozarrela Tortelloni

Description

My favortie sunday dinner. I eat it until I feel sick.

Ingredients

  • Red onion
  • Store Pasta
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Pepper
  • Crushed Red Pepper
  • Lemon Juice

Steps

  1. Bring water to boil
  2. While water is boiling, chop onion
  3. When water is boiling, empty package of pasta into boiling water
  4. Boil posta for five minutes
  5. Drain pasta
  6. Cook onions in butter
  7. Combine all ingredients, season to taste, and toss them together.
